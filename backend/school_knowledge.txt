School Overview
Korea International School navigation map includes various connected destinations such as the: Main Entrance, Soccer Field, Elementary School, Play Spaces, Cafeteria, Lower Hill, Middle School, Secondary Library, Performing Arts, High School, Outdoor Field, G Building, and Bus Area.

Main Entrance
The Main Entrance is the primary arrival point for visitors and families. It connects directly toward Elementary School and the Cafeteria paths.

Soccer Field
The Soccer Field is the main outdoor pitch for PE, team practices, and interschool matches. Typical after-school use includes soccer training, conditioning drills, and seasonal team tryouts.

Elementary School
Elementary School classrooms are grouped by grade-level homerooms and shared learning spaces.

Play Spaces
Play Spaces include outdoor recreation areas for break time and elementary activities. These areas are connected to both the Soccer Field side and Middle School side routes.

Cafeteria
The Cafeteria is a central lunch and gathering area for students and staff. It is a major routing hub that connects toward Main Entrance, Elementary School, Secondary Library, and Lower Hill.

Lower Hill
Lower Hill is the sloped transition route that links the central campus to the Performing Arts side. It is primarily used as a walking connector.

Middle School
Middle School contains core academic classrooms and advisory spaces. Example classroom references include M101, M202, and M305.

Secondary Library
Secondary Library supports research, study blocks, and collaborative work time. Nearby classroom-style references can include H201 and H202 seminar spaces.

Performing Arts
Performing Arts includes rehearsal and performance rooms used for drama, music, and school productions. This zone connects to Lower Hill, Secondary Library, and Outdoor Field routes.

High School
High School contains upper-grade classrooms and labs. Common classroom references include H101, H203, and H305.

Outdoor Field
Outdoor Field is a general multi-use field area for PE, sports conditioning, and large outdoor activities.

G Building
G Building is an academic building often used for specialized classes and small group sessions. Common classroom references include G301, G402, and G201.

Bus Area
Bus Area is the transportation pickup and drop-off zone. It is typically the endpoint for dismissal routing.

Classroom To Building Reference
M101, M202, and M305 are in Middle School. H101, H203, and H305 are in High School. G301, G102, and G201 are in G Building. H201 and H202 are in Secondary Library.

Event Notes
Soccer Field events usually include PE classes during school hours and soccer-related activities after school. Outdoor Field events may include assemblies, athletics, and seasonal school programs. Event schedules can change, so students should confirm exact times with school announcements.
